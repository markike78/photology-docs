## fal.ai Dashboard - Set up Billing

10. Once you are on the fal.ai Dashboard page, click the **Set up billing** button next to "Add a payment method"<br>
**NOTE: ** This is not some kind of recurring billing or trial membership that you have to remember how to cancel.  You can easily add a specific amount one time and never use
it again or get charged again if you don't want to.  That's how I use it.<br>
<img width="425" height="402" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94f27d64-505e-4611-a140-5b09f4f96a82" />

<br><br>

11. Enter your credit card or bank info.  
** You are not being billed at this time.**  <br>

12. Enter your address Billing Address
<br>

13. For **Tax Estimate** on the right side, enter **$0**.  This is for people using this as a business.<br>
14. Click **Save**

## Add Credits

15. Go back to the home page dashboard by clicking **Home** in the upper left of the page
16. Now you will **Add Credits**<br>
<img width="324" height="296" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fef61c05-79c5-4e85-b468-cb9aa84bec09" />

<br><br>

17. Under **Add Credits**, I recommend starting with **$10**<br>
18. Set the **"Top-up mount"** and the **"When balance falls below"** amounts to $0<br>
19. Click **Quick Buy $10** (or whatever amount you authorized) <br>
<img width="1480" height="542" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ad6f028-4022-4750-b4a4-17fb2635fc94" />

<br><br>

20. Confirm your purchase<br>
<img width="279" height="175" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2571b95b-d132-495a-9b39-c2933119ed38" />

21. Wait for the processing to complete.<br>
22. You will be redirected back to the billing page and see that you have a Current balance now available!<br>
<img width="504" height="144" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf0aade8-a224-4b54-be41-8d9d21a7b835" />
